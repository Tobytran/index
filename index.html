Our facilities

We offer world-class facilities. Modern classrooms, purpose-built lecture theatres, studios, laboratories and other specialist spaces provide the perfect setting to thrive.

Wold Class Library

Our libraries provide access to over 300,000 books, periodicals, e-books and e-journals. Students and alumni also have online access to RMIT’s extensive library in Australia. Our online learning includes access to LinkedIn Learning.

lagrge play ground

You will have access to world-class facilities at our London campus including: outdoor playing fields, a fully-equipped gym, and an indoor multi-purpose sports complex. You will also have access to a range of exercise and sport clubs. Our New York campus has a multi-purpose REC room which includes a range of fitness and recreation activities.

taste and healthy food

At Whashington campus, there are over ten food and beverage options for students, covering Vietnamese and international cuisines, as well as a wide variety of drinks and snacks, open all day. The open air dining area has been recently refurbished, offering the perfect space for socialising and meetings.
